Mendoza Reads seeks to create a sense of community among local readers, inviting them to share and enjoy reading in an inclusive and accessible space.

Domain

mendoza-reads.org

Site Purpose

Mendoza Reads is a digital plataform dedicated to connecting book lovers in Mendoza. Its purpose is to create a space where members can discover new books, participate in literary discussions, share reviews, and be part of a vibrant book club. The site seeks to encourage reading, promote local literary culture, and strengthen the sense of community among readers.

Scenarios

  • How can I join Mendoza Reads and participate in the reading clubs?
  • Where can I find book recommendations from local or regional authors?
