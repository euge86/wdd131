Mendoza, Argentina

Mendoza is a city in western Argentina and capital of the province of the same name . It is located on the plain east of the Andes mountain range. It is one of the main cities in the country, and with its urban aglomeration, called Greater Mendoza, it reaches a total population of over one million inhabitants. Its surface area is 57 km², although its metropolitan area extends to 168 km².
